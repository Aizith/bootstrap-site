How to help the Polar Bears

There is plenty that we can do to help these animals in the north. Here are six simple ways to help them.

  • Car Pooling and using less toxic fumes
  • Dispose of your waste properly
  • Spread the word to others
  • Do not dump toxic waste
  • Support the research by volunteering for the cause
  • If you don't have time to volunteer there is always donations. They go a distance aswell
